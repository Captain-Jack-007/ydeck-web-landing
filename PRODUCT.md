# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

YDeck is aimed at founders, educators, consultants, companies, and organizations who create professional presentations from private source material. At an expo, visitors are scanning quickly and need to understand the product, trust the privacy promise, and join the pilot without exploring a full dashboard.

## Product Purpose

YDeck turns prompts, PDFs, books, reports, lesson material, and company documents into structured, branded presentations. The landing page exists to communicate the private/local-first AI agent promise, demonstrate the path from source material to editable PPTX/PDF, and convert qualified visitors into pilot waitlist signups.

## Brand Personality

Professional, secure, precise. The brand should feel like a serious presentation workstation: calm enough for confidential business material, sharp enough for investor and training decks, and concrete enough to be understood in seconds at a booth.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS visuals, vague purple/blue AI gradients, inflated fake metrics, abstract productivity claims, and repeated card grids. Avoid implying social proof, customer counts, compliance status, or security certifications that the product has not earned.

## Design Principles

Make the product category obvious within five seconds.

Make privacy the dominant proof, not a footnote.

Show the transformation from source material to structured deck.

Prefer concrete evidence over hype.

Keep the waitlist path always available but never desperate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation and forms, clear focus states, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts for booth phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop displays.
