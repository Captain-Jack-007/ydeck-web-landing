## Why landing page is better for the expo

At an expo, people usually do not have time to explore a full dashboard. They need to understand YDeck in 5–10 seconds.

Your goal is not to make them use the whole product immediately. Your goal is:

```text
1. Understand the product
2. Feel the value
3. Trust the privacy promise
4. Join the pilot/waitlist
```

So the landing page should be built for conversion.
